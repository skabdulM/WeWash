  • Understanding the laundry business scenario in India
  • To plan a business module, Capex, Opex and ROI
  • Complete transparency throughout the setting up of your facility
  • Complete training on handling the store, chemical usage, processing garments, processing leather articles, stock inventory, Stock Requisition, Staff management, Software etc.
  • Complete assistance and help during the process of finalizing the store location. Checking the feasibility of your preferred store location across multiple parameters.
  • Detailed advise on laundry machines and customizing the set of machines for your facility.
  • Set up of industrial electric, water, steam, air inlets and outlets
  • Procurement of machines and equipments
  • Furniture and fixtures for the store
  • Laundry software
  • Store painting
  • Facia
  • Advise for marketing and advertisement plan
  • Assistance in store handling and management
